COMPANY PROFILE

The journey of Agro Spice Food Packers's success starts from 11th March 1993 when the dream of one man became a reality! With the hard work of all the employees and other well-wishers now the company has become one of the top manufacturers of processed foods, especially Sri Lankan ethnic foods for both export and local markets. This is a private, independent company under the Food processing sector. Our headquarters is in Thalawathugoda. At present Agro Spice Food Packers export products to Australia, USA, UK, UAE, New Zealand etc. under their "AGRO" brand name.
